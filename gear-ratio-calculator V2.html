Key Terms & Definitions


        
Torque

        A turning force applied to a shaft or gear, measured in Newton metres (Nm).


        
RPM

        Revolutions Per Minute. The number of complete rotations made in one minute.


        
Clockwise (CW)

        Rotation in the same direction as the hands of a clock.


        
Anti-Clockwise (ACW)

        Rotation opposite to the direction of the hands of a clock.
